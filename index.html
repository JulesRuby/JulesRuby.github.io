J.R. Muri is a Calgary based front end developer fascinated with creating aesthetically pleasing, fun user interfaces and experiences. Always looking to learn and apply new skills for innovative solutions!

While he most relishes exploring client facing technologies like Vue, Javascript and CSS3/Sass; he also has experience with Node.JS, Express, MongoDB, Python and Flask.

A lot of his work, designs and color-use are heavily influenced by his love of video games and photography. Which are two ways he spends his free time bombarding his retinas with photons, when not coding. The last way is by staring directly at the sun... What’s that jerk hiding, anyway?
